Privacy Policy for Quirk Squad

At Quirk Squad, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data while you use our game.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the following information from our users:

  • Email address – Used to access your game data.
  • Username – Chosen by you during the registration process.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your email address is used solely to allow you to access your game data stored in our database. We do not use it for any other purpose, such as sending notifications or marketing materials.

3. How We Store Your Information

Your email and username are stored locally on your device in an SQLite database. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted or stored on our servers.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not share, sell, or transfer your email address or any other personal data to third parties. Your personal data is only used to manage your game experience.

5. Data Retention

Your email and username are stored indefinitely on your device until you decide to cancel your account. If you choose to delete your account, all personal information will be removed from the database.

6. Data Protection

Currently, we do not implement any encryption or access control mechanisms for the data stored on your device. However, we are committed to continually improving our data protection practices.

7. User Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated personal data. To do so, you can delete your account directly within the game, and all personal information will be removed from the local database.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our game is intended for users who are 13 years or older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If we learn that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will delete that information as soon as possible.

9. Legal Compliance

We aim to comply with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Union. If you are located outside of the EU, please be aware that we do not transfer data across borders.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
